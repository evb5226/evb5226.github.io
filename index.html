You Are Not Alone

Studies show that college is one of the most stressful times in a person's life. A person is expected to get good grades, keep up social appearances, and sleep. A survey conducted by Associated Press found that 80 percent of students say that they frequently or sometimes experience daily stress....34 percent of students have felt depressed at some point in the last 3 months...and 9 percent have seriously considered suicide...

Why Pets for College?

Animals are relaxing and cute. They are a friend when we are in need. Often times merely making an animal happy makes people happy. Throughout the semester The University Health Services brings puppies on campus to help students de-stress, but what about all the other days of the year? Can students access the love and compassion of animals at all times while in school? YES! Pets for College allows students to interact with pets virtually at any place and any time.
